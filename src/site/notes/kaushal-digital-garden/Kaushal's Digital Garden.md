## Who am I?
My name is Kaushal Sheth, and I currently reside in California. My career has been focused on data since the very start, so I have [[kaushal-digital-garden/Data/Data Home\|many opinions and thoughts on that topic.]] I've always been interested in [[kaushal-digital-garden/Finance/Finance Home\|personal finance]], which includes investing, planning for retirement, options trading and teaching my kids about managing their money. Lately, I've started focusing a lot on my [[kaushal-digital-garden/Health/Health Home\|health]], mainly around losing weight and getting more fit. I've only started exploring this area recently, so I have a long way to go here. And last, but certainly not least, I'm passionate about [[kaushal-digital-garden/Singing/Singing Home\|singing]] - I try to spend at least 2-3 hours each day singing - mostly Bollywood, but also some English songs. Of course, I have other interests, but for now, I plan to write about these topics, and may very well expand in the future.
